Yummy Food Generator

This is where you can play around and post some fun foods onto the canvas! Make a cute beige/brown themed palette with the buttons. Save your creations with the "Save Image" button, and if you want a different canvas color, use the "Random New Screen" button to get a random color background! Or if you want to stick to white, just use the regular "Clear Screen" button! Click the screen if you want to add a secret food...
